Why work with us?

Great atmosphere

Codespace is a collaborative work environment. We strive to make all our team members feel comfortable, effective, free, and valued. You will take on responsibilities from the start. We count on every team member to contribute ideas. Here, you will develop professionally surrounded by other professionals of the tech sector. At codespace, you will have opportunities for promotion.

Plenty of experience

We are a part of a business group with 30+ years of experience and over 300 employees, but we are also independent. We embarked on this ambitious project, and you will be a fundamental part of our journey. To achieve success, it is clear that we need to form the best team possible: your knowledge and skills will be crucial in helping us achieve our goals together, and to build our future.

Always up-to-date

We are passionate about technology at codespace. Our philosophy is not to only work tirelessly, but to create opportunities for our team members to investigate, train, and discover new professional challenges every day!
