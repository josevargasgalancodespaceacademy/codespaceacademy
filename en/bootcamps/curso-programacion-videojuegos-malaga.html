Developing your own VIDEO GAME, it’s not just a game

Immerse yourself in our training campus to become a video game programmer, in only 12 weeks. Acquire skills in some of the most demanded and current technologies through a learning by doing training model, where you will learn to program by programming.

Bootcamp specially designed for:

  • People passionate about video games and with a desire to learn to create video games, but have little to no experience, using Unity 5 programming software.

  • Creative profiles with the intention of enhancing professional skills and acquire technical aspects of programming.

  • People interested in knowing the bases and foundations for 2D and 3D video games.

  • People with basic knowledge of C# programming, Java, or another similar programming language.

Why program with Unity 5?

Unity is a very popular open-source multiplatform video game engine that works as a development platform for Microsoft Windows, iOS X, Linux, Android, consoles, and virtual reality devices.
Video games like Life is Strange, Pokémon Go, Angry Birds, and 7 days to die have been developed using Unity.

Initial module – 3 weeks

You will be introduced to the building blocks on which we will base the development of the Video Games Development Bootcamp.

To get you started we will include essential aspects such as mathematical thinking, physics knowledge, introduction to programming, exploring code, and structured work. This will all translate into your first 2D video game, a platform game. C# language...

  • Mathematics for developers

    • Algebra, geometry, physics, probability, numbering

  • Introduction to programming in C#

    • Data types, arrays, loops, decisions …

  • Introduction to Unity 2D

    • Complete 2D game (jumping rabbit that collects coins)

  • Tools and work methodology for developers

    • Workshops classes/Workshops on real cases (Polo/PTA)

Intermediate module – 5 weeks

We will consolidate the previous fundamentals and apply them to develop a 3D game: including characters and animations, lighting scenarios, gameplay programming, graphical user interfaces, and experimenting on game mechanics with game design analysis.

  • Creating 3D scenarios

    • Scenarios, textures, rendering, audio, lighting, natural effects…

  • Developing Gameplays

    • Trajectories, forces, torsions

  • Animating in Unity: Characters and Animations

    • Animations, rigging, transitions, triggers, state machines, animation programming…

  • Creating graphical user interfaces

    • Canvas and Event Systems, elements of UI, layout, programming and interaction, mini map…

  • Introduction to Game Design
2

never dead - level up

Advanced module – 4 weeks

Put the finishing touches to your game with artificial intelligence. Turn your game into multiplayer or launch the next virtual reality game. Additionally, you will learn to key steps to turn your game into a business, monetize it, and get rid of bugs before launching your product into production.

  • Introduction to Unreal Engine
  • Introduction to Virtual Reality
  • Artificial Intelligence, advanced algorithms, and multiplayer

    • Trees, path algorithms, pathfinding, waypoint systems, decision making, fuzzy logic, dynamic learning systems, neural networks, synchronous and asynchronous games, real-time connection…

  • Turning your video game into a business (Business, KPI, monetization…)
  • Data analysis
  • Scrum Methodologies
  • QA Testing
