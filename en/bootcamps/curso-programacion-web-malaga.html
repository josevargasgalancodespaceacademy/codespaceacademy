Who is it for?

  • People without previous training

    If you have always wanted to enter the world of technology, but have never know how, this is your chance. Codespace puts at your disposal all possible means, from scratch, to become a professional in a matter of months.

  • Recently graduated

    If you just finished secondary school, or have recently graduated and want to continue training, codespace puts to your disposal all the possible tools to launch your career.

  • Recycling

    Have you been programming for years but find yourself falling behind? Codespace provides you the opportunity to recycle your knowledge in a matter of moths with the aim of continuing the professional front.

  • Feeling a career change

    Do you want to change your career to something more technical? Do you see that your career has stagnated at a professional level? The codespace Bootcamp is designed so that you learn the skills and knowledge necessary to obtain a job as a Full-Stack web developer or any related technical position.

Structure of the bootcamp

Weeks 1 to 4

Prework

The first thing is to change your way of thinking! You will learn to initiate patters of logical thinking, information comprehension, technological learning skills, communication, problem solving skills, productivity, adaptability, code exploration, and structured work.

Once you acquire these skills, you will learn the basic knowledge of programming, relational databases and dynamic web design. All using HTML, CSS, MySQL, and JavaScript. You will also learn how to host your projects on GitHub.

  • Programming methodology.
  • Introduction to BBDD.
  • Introduction to web development.
  • Introduction to programming

1

Weeks 5 to 10

Module 1

Basic Front and Back

Once you have achieved a solid foundation, you will create an attractive and dynamic user experience with HTML5 and CSS3; as well as, deepen JavaScript, using Ecmascript6 and libraries like Jquery, going deep in advanced algorithms and in the back-end of a web learning PHP.

You will also understand the differences between SQL and NoSQL. Finally, we will continue to deepen Git and GitHup, along with English classes specialized in the sector.

  • Advanced DB: SQL and NoSQL
  • HTML5 and CSS3.
  • Ecmascript6, Jquery.
  • Algorithms and data structures.
  • English.
  • Project 1.

2

Weeks 11 to 13

Module 2

APIS Rest & Web Services

Development of applications based on non-relational databases using Node.js, ExpressJS, and MongoDB. In addition, you will learn to create your own APIS and implement Web Services with AJAX.
Continue to receive English classes specialized in the sector.

  • Node JS. and Express JS.
  • MongoDB.
  • Design of APIS. REST.
  • Web Services with AJAX.
  • English.
  • Project 2.

3

Weeks 14 to 16

Module 3

Full Stack Development

Finally, you will gain knowledge to create applications with React and Redux and learn how to work with parcel systems. On the other hand, you will learn Scrum methodologies and how to launch your application to a production environment from scratch.

Accompanied by English classes in the sector.

  • React, Redux.
  • Parcel systems: Webpack.
  • Full Stack: from 0 to production.
  • Project management: Scrum.
  • English.

4

Weeks 17 to 19

Final Project

Everything you learned during the Bootcamp will be reflected in your own project, advised by the entire teaching council and mentored by leading experts in the sector.

You will present your final project in the codespace facilities before mentors, companies, family, and friends.

5

Week 20

Hiring Week!

The last phase! You will work with the codespace technical team in solving real cases, conducting technical interviews, and you will enter the network of partner companies, showing your skills to our network of partner companies.

6

F.A.Q.

  • What is a Coding Bootcamp? +

    • If we take the dictionary definition of Bootcamp, it is a training camp. Therefore, a Coding Bootcamp is simply an immersive training, at a high intensity, over a short period of time to learn how to program while programming. That is to say, it is a “learn by doing” model of training. On the other hand, our priority is to promote our golden rule, “simply, do it”, that is, prepare the student to face any type of problem in the field of programming, and have the ability to analyze, solve, and develop it. All this guarantees you will be fully prepared to jump into the professional world in a matter of months.

  • Why make a Coding Bootcamp? +

    • Deciding on a bootcamp as an option to learn programming can raise many questions. All this is normal because it is a significant economic investment and an intense effort for a few months. How do you know it is the right option? If we take into account that the average duration of an online course you would do at home is about 32 weeks (about 8 months), and that only 5% of students complete an online course on their own, students of our Bootcamps would have finished and been working with a salary for several months, over that same time period. Also, completing a Bootcamp is very profitable. The vast majority of bootcamp graduates find employment, and 73% of graduates do so with employment that required the skills learned. Lastly, the employment bootcamp graduates find, have an increase in salary on average of 64%. Codespace, guarantees graduate students employment with one of our partner companies.
